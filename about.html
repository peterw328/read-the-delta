About

What this site is and why it exists.

Purpose

Read the Delta is a reference site for U.S. economic data. It tracks key indicators and explains what changed since the last official release.

The goal is orientation, not analysis. The site answers one question: what is different now?

Editorial Approach

Content is factual and neutral. Headlines describe changes without characterizing them as good or bad. No predictions are made. No investment advice is offered.

The tone is institutional. Think government reference publication, not financial news.

Coverage

The site currently covers:

  • U.S. Jobs Report (monthly)
  • Inflation Report (monthly)
